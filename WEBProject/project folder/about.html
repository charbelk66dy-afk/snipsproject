About Snips

Who We Are

Snips is one of Lebanon’s most beloved baked snack brands, made proudly in the Bekaa Valley by Daher Foods. With decades of experience in the food industry, our mission has always been clear: deliver bold flavors, quality ingredients, and a crunch that everyone can enjoy.

Our production facilities use modern baking technology to craft snacks that are lighter, crispier, and healthier than traditional fried chips. The Snips family includes a wide range of flavors—from spicy to cheesy to classic—and continues to expand every year.

Behind every bag of Snips is a dedicated team committed to excellence, innovation, and consistency. We value our customers, our employees, and the community we serve.
